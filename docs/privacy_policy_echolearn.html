Privacy Policy

EchoLearn respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how your data is handled within the app.

Data Collection

EchoLearn does not collect, transmit, or store personal information on external servers by default. All data you enter, including imported questions, recorded voice notes, and test results, remains securely on your own device unless you explicitly choose to enable cloud features.

Data Usage

Your information is used solely to provide you with the functionality of learning, testing, and progress tracking. No data is shared with third parties. Voice recordings and test results are processed locally to deliver features such as playback, reflection, and topic tracking.

AI Integration

EchoLearn offers optional integration with OpenAI GPT. If you link your account, prompts and questions may be sent to OpenAI’s servers for processing. EchoLearn does not store or share your GPT account credentials. You remain in control of whether to use this feature.

Future Cloud Services

In future versions, optional cloud synchronization may be introduced using Apple’s CloudKit or other secure services. This will only occur with your explicit consent. You will be notified and given the choice before any data is uploaded or shared.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated as new features are added. Any changes will be communicated clearly within the app, and your continued use will signify acceptance of the updated terms.

Contact
