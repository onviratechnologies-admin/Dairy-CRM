Privacy Policy

Onvira Parent Hub respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how your data is handled within the app.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers without your consent. All data you access, including fee details, homework, transport information, queries, leave requests, and school notices, remains securely managed within the app and your school’s authorized systems.

Data Usage

Your information is used solely to provide you with the functionality of tracking your child’s school details and communicating with the institution. No data is shared with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Initial Version

This is the initial version of Onvira Parent Hub. Lots of new features will be coming in future updates to enhance your experience and provide more value. We are committed to continuous improvement.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated as new features are added. Any changes will be communicated clearly within the app, and your continued use will signify acceptance of the updated terms.
