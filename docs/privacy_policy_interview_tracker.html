Privacy Policy

Interview Tracker respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how your data is handled within the app.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data you enter, including company details, interview progress, and notes, remains securely on your own device.

Data Usage

Your information is used solely to provide you with the functionality of tracking interviews and managing your job search process. No data is shared with third parties.

Future Cloud Services

In future versions, optional cloud synchronization may be introduced using Apple’s CloudKit. This will only occur with your explicit consent. You will be notified and given the choice before any data is uploaded or shared.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated as new features are added. Any changes will be communicated clearly within the app, and your continued use will signify acceptance of the updated terms.
